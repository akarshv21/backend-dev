
Q1. Online Banking System 
answer: SQL Database
. Requires ACID properties  ensures reliable transactions
. Needs high data consistency no errors in money transfer
. Data is structured (accounts, transactions)
. Supports security and constraints
  Justification: Banking systems cannot tolerate errors, so SQL is best.


Q2. Social Media Platform 
Answer: NoSQL Database
. Handles large unstructured data (posts, images, videos)
. Requires high scalability** (millions of users)
. Schema is flexible and dynamic**
. Provides high availability**
Justification: NoSQL efficiently manages big and changing data.

Q3. Real-time Chat Application 
Answer: NoSQL Database
. Needs fast read/write operations
. Handles high volume of messages
. Requires low latency (real-time delivery)
. Flexible data model for chats
  Justification: NoSQL supports speed and scalability needed for chat apps.


Q4. Hospital Patient Records 
Answer: SQL Database
. Requires high accuracy and consistency.
. Data is structured (patients, reports)
. Needs strong security and privacy
. Maintains relationships between data
Justification: SQL ensures reliable and secure medical records.



Q5. IoT Sensor Data Collection 
Answer: NoSQL Database
. Handles massive continuous data streams
. Data is semi-structured
.Needs high scalability and performance
.Supports distributed systems
 Justification: NoSQL is ideal for handling large, fast IoT data.
